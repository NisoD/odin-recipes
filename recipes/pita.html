Homemade Pita Bread

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (about 110°F/43°C)
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water. Let sit for 5–10 minutes until frothy.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms. Knead on a floured surface for about 10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  3. Place dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover, and let rise in a warm place for 1–1.5 hours, until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). Place a baking stone or tray inside to heat.
  5. Punch down the dough and divide into 8 equal pieces. Roll each into a ball and then into a 6-inch round.
  6. Place 2–3 rounds at a time on the hot baking surface. Bake for 2–3 minutes or until they puff up and are lightly golden.
  7. Remove and cover with a clean towel to keep warm. Repeat with remaining dough.
